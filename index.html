Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a dress code?
Formal attire. We recommend a layer or wrap for the outdoor ceremony, as late-October evenings in the Hudson Valley get cool.
Are kids welcome?
Yes! Kids are welcome at every part of the weekend, from the rehearsal dinner through the after-party.
Is parking available on-site, or is there a shuttle?
Parking is available on-site at Cedar Lakes Estate. We'll also be running a shuttle from Newark Airport to the hotel.Shuttle schedule TBD — add times once confirmed
Is the ceremony indoors or outdoors, and what's the rain plan?
The ceremony will be outdoors on the estate grounds. If weather doesn't cooperate, we'll move indoors — either way, the celebration goes on.
Can I bring a plus-one who wasn't on my invitation?
We're only able to accommodate the guests named on your invitation. Thank you for understanding!
Will dietary restrictions and allergies be accommodated?
Yes — just let us know any dietary restrictions or allergies ahead of time (on your RSVP) and we'll make sure you're taken care of.
What time should I arrive, and how do I get back at the end of the night?
Plan to arrive Friday morning and depart Sunday midday. More detail on shuttle/transportation timing to come.Add shuttle/checkout logistics once confirmed
Is the after-party open to everyone, including kids?
Yes — every part of the wedding weekend, including the after-party, is open to guests of all ages.
What's the best way to celebrate with a gift?
We ask that gifts come through our registry only — please, no gifts in person. You'll find the registry link below.
Anything special we should pack?
If you'd like to use the estate's amenities, pack workout and swim gear — Cedar Lakes has a gym, pool, and lake access on the grounds.
